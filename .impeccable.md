## Design Context

### Users
Gift-givers looking for personalized recommendations — often stressed, short on time, want to find something meaningful without endless scrolling. They arrive from organic search (Google), typically on mobile, and need quick, trustworthy results. International audience across 8 countries (DE, ES, UK, US, CA, FR, IT, NL).

### Brand Personality
**Warm, personal, helpful** — like getting gift advice from a thoughtful friend, not from an algorithm. The AI should feel invisible; the experience should feel human.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Away from:** Purple/pink AI gradients, shimmer animations, sparkle effects, generic "AI tool" aesthetic, Lovable template look
- **Toward:** Warm, inviting, personal — like a cozy gift shop, not a tech startup
- **Color palette:**
  - Primary: Sage green (#7C9082) — calming, natural, trustworthy
  - Accent: Soft apricot/peach (#F4A261) — warm, friendly, inviting
  - Background: Warm cream (#FDF8F4) — soft, not clinical white
  - Text: Warm charcoal (#3D3D3D) — readable, not harsh black
  - Secondary: Dusty rose (#D4A0A0) — subtle warmth for highlights
  - Success/Match: Muted gold (#C9A96E) — for "perfect match" indicators
- **Typography:** Rounded, friendly sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Nunito). No sharp geometric fonts.
- **Theme:** Light mode only. Warm and airy.
- **Imagery:** Minimal, purposeful. No decorative AI-themed graphics. If icons, use simple line icons.
- **Animations:** Subtle and purposeful only — gentle fade-ins on page transitions, smooth hover states. No shimmer, sparkle, float, pulse, or any "AI thinking" animations.

### Design Principles
1. **Invisible AI** — The technology powers the experience but never shows itself. No "AI-powered" badges, no loading animations that look like neural networks. Just helpful results.
2. **Warm minimalism** — Clean and spacious, but never cold. Every element should feel intentional and welcoming.
3. **Trust through simplicity** — Users are about to click affiliate links. Clean design, clear product cards, honest pricing build trust. No visual tricks.
4. **Mobile-first** — Most traffic comes from search. The quiz and results must work beautifully on phones.
5. **Content over chrome** — The gift recommendations are the hero. UI should frame them, not compete with them.
